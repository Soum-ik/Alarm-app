Why it works

🔳

Scan to dismiss

Register something you own — a water bottle, a book, a barcode across the room. The alarm stops only when you scan that exact object.

🔌

Charger gate

It also won't stop until the phone is plugged in — so you can't silence it from bed.

🚫

No snooze

There's no snooze button. The only escape is a 30-minute emergency backstop.

🔁

Survives reboot

Re-arms after a restart or app kill. It's reliable, but it never fights uninstall — you stay in control.

How to install

Alearm installs directly from this page (it's not on the Play Store yet), so Android will ask you to confirm a few things. This is normal for direct downloads.

Download the APK

Tap the Download for Android button above on your phone.

Allow the install

Open the downloaded file. If Android warns about “unknown sources”, tap Settings → Allow from this source, then go back.

Confirm & install

Dismiss the Play Protect scan prompt if it appears, then tap Install.

Grant permissions

Open Alearm and allow Camera (to scan objects) and Alarms & notifications so it can wake you.

Heads up: Alearm is Android-only. It cannot be installed on an iPhone or iPad — Apple doesn't allow apps from outside the App Store.
